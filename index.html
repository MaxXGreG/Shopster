Shopster, Your Online Store Companion

Hello, this web site is very simple, to create an item and put it on the shoping list press the small plus at the bottom and enter the name, amount and metric of the desired item. When you want to mark it as bought press the blue check icon and if you desire to see everything that you bought, you can see all bought items if you click the green check icon at the bottom left, if you want to clear the whole "bought" section click the clear button in it.
